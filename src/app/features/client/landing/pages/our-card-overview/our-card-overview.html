How to get the card?

Becoming an owner of the Platform card is easy! Just follow a few steps:

1

Step 1: Check the basic requirements

Before applying for the Platform card registration, make sure you have activated 2FA in your exchange account, verified your identity, and have the first Holding level.

2

Step 2: Complete quick verification

To apply for a card, you need a passport or residence permit. Having one of these documents will help you pass the verification stage quickly.

3

Step 3: Apply for KYC at PayUnicard

Complete the necessary steps to register the card on the PayUnicard website. You will need to provide personal information and wait for the result.

4

Step 4: Download the PayUnicard app

Download the PayUnicard app to check the application status and manage your Platform card.

5

Step 5: Done! The Platform card is at your service

The Platform card supports USD, EUR and GBP.
The Platform card allows withdrawals in USD, EUR and GBP.
To do this, you need to specify your PayUnicard account nickname when withdrawing. With PayUnicard, you can withdraw only USD, EUR and GBP Additionally, USD can be withdrawn using Checkout.
To link Platform and PayUnicard accounts, additional verification is required. For additional verification you need to provide one of the following documents: passport or residence permit. If your application is rejected, your verification status on the exchange will remain unchanged.
When ordering the card, you chose one of the available tariff plans. Transfer the amount provided by the selected plan to your PayUnicard account. This amount will be automatically credited as payment for your Platform card tariff plan.
In your PayUnicard account, you can change the PIN code of the Platform card. To do this, log in to your PayUnicard account, select the Platform card, and click the "Change Card PIN" button. A message with a new PIN code will be sent to your mobile number. You can also add the card number to the Whitelist. To do this, hover over the account icon on the exchange and select the "Security" section. In the "Address Management" block, change the status to "Whitelist enabled" and add the card number.
